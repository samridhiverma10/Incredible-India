Located in the Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra, Shirdi has emerged as a pilgrimage destination of top order due to its association with the renowned mystic Saint Shri Sai Baba. Revered by millions of people across the world, Shri Sai Baba preached tolerance towards all religions and the message of universal brotherhood. A world in itself, Shirdi is one of the busiest pilgrim centers in the country. Every activity at Shirdi revolves around the vast temple complex dedicated to Sai Baba. Other places of importance are the Gurusthan, Khandoba Temple, Shani Mandir, Narsimha Mandir, Changdev Maharaj Samadhi Sakori Ashram, and Chavadi, and Dwarkamai.
